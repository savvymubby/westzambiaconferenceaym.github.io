About WZC Adventist Youth Movement

West Zambia Conference-Adventist Youth Ministries covers the western part of Zambia with eighteen(18) districts which include Mongu Central, Mongu North, Mongu South, Kalabo Central, Kaoma Central, Kaoma West, Nkeyema, Senanga, Sesheke West, Sesheke East, Mulobezi, Sitoti, Shangombo, Sikongo, Liumba, Siluwe, Zambezi East, and Lukulu.


AY MISSION:

To lead young people into a saving relationship with Jesus Christ and help them embrace His call to discipleship


AY AIM:

The Advent message to all the world in my generation.


AY MOTTO:

The love of Christ compels me.


AY PLEDGE:

Loving the Lord Jesus, I promise to take an active part in the youth ministry of the church, doing what I can to help others and to finish the work of the Gospel in all the world.
