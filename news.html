Dear Youth,

As you walk through the highs and lows of this life, remember your Creator.
God wants to use you in His vineyard to bring lost souls into His Kingdom.
Do not waste your strength and efforts on things that profit you nothing but
rather lead you away from your God. Now is the time to wake up and do your
best for Christ Jesus our Lord, Amen.
